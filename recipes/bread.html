Bread

How to make such a nice piece of Bread!

With these simple steps this lasagna could be yours!

Well.. not exactly this one, that would be gross

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast

Step by Step

  1. Mix water and yeast
  2. Mix water with flour
  3. Let it rest for 1 hour
  4. Shape into a bread-like shape
  5. Put in the oven at 200 degress for 20 minutes
  6. Enjoy in moderation
