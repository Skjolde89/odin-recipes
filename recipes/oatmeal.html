Lasagna

How to make such a nice bowl of Oatmeal!

With these simple steps this lasagna could be yours!

Well.. not exactly this one, that would be gross

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Water
  • Salt

Step by Step

  1. Mix water and oatmeal in a pot
  2. Medium heat
  3. Stir
  4. Add salt
  5. Eat with ton of butter on top
  6. Enjoy!
